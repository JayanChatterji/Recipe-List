Gulab Jamun

Integrents

  • Khoya
  • Maida
  • Baking soda
  • Milk
  • Ghee
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Cardamom
  • Rose Water

Recipe

  1. In a pan, add sugar and water.
  2. Heat until sugar dissolves, then add crushed cardamom and saffron (optional).
  3. Boil for 5–7 minutes until slightly sticky, then add rose/kewra water and keep warm.
  4. In a bowl, mash khoya until smooth.
  5. Add maida, baking soda, and semolina (optional) and mix gently.
  6. Add milk little by little to make a soft, smooth dough.
  7. Rest the dough for 5–10 minutes.
  8. Make small smooth balls without cracks.
  9. Heat oil/ghee on medium-low flame and fry balls until golden brown.
  10. Remove from oil and immediately soak in warm sugar syrup.
  11. Let them soak for 1–2 hours before serving.

Barfi

Integrents

  • Khoya
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom powder
  • Ghee
  • Chopped nuts
  • Rose water

Recipe

  1. Heat a pan on low flame and add khoya.
  2. Stir continuously until it softens and releases some fat.
  3. Add sugar and mix well.
  4. Cook the mixture until it thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  5. Add cardamom powder and mix.
  6. Add rose/kewra water for flavor.
  7. Grease a plate with ghee and pour the mixture into it.
  8. Spread evenly and smooth the top.
  9. Garnish with chopped nuts.
  10. Let it cool completely, then cut into squares or diamonds.
